Dear Aarna,
I'm truly sorry, even though I know that words can't always undo what has been done. I sincerely apologize for the careless events of that day, which had no intention of bringing about the current situation. Someone who genuinely respected you didn't deserve to show you such recklessness. The loss of your presence leads me to guilt I doubt I can loose.

"Some souls meet for a reason, even if it's only for a short season," they say. I'm not sure how, but you were my storm shelter; without having to try, you made me feel at ease and as though the world wasn't as heavy as I had assumed. Even though ten days might not seem like much, they held greater significance than I can adequately convey. I want you to know that some people don't just enter your life, they ease the weight of it. I'm not asking for your friendship or forgiveness right away, understanding the situation you are in currently as well. I will always be thankful for what you did for me, but I also regret that I wont be able relive the experience a day more.

I am fully aware of and respectful of your need for time. I just hope that we can talk again when things feel right, but that's all I'm asking for right now. The simplicity of our conversations and the tranquility they provided are already making me nostalgic. I'll give you the space you requested and sincerely wish you well until then.
